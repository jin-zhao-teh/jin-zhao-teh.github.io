Physics Models

Equations and formulas used in the simulation.

This simulation models fusion reactor physics using the following equations:

  • Fusion reaction rates for DT, DHe3, and DD fuels
  • Bremsstrahlung, synchrotron, and line radiation losses
  • Gyro-Bohm and neoclassical transport models
  • Energy balance with alpha particle heating
  • Plasma stability criteria (nτT, beta limit)

Fusion Cross Section

For DT fuel (Hively's formula):

σv = 1.1e-24 * T2 / (1 + 0.137*T2/3) * exp(-19.94/T1/3)

For DHe3 fuel (Bosch-Hale):

σv = 5.6e-21 * T-2/3 * exp(-58.3 * T-1/3)

Radiation Losses

Bremsstrahlung radiation:

Pbrem = 5.34e-37 * Zeff * ne² * √T

Synchrotron radiation:

Psyn = 6.21e-23 * B2 * T * ne * R

Transport Models

Gyro-Bohm diffusion coefficient:

DGB = 0.1 * T1.5 / B

Neoclassical transport:

DNC = 3.4e-3 * √A * Z2 * n * T-0.5 / B2

Stability Criteria

Lawson Criterion:

nτET > 3e21 keV·s·m-3 (DT)

Beta limit:

β = 2μ0nT / B2 < βmax
